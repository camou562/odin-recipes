Egg Scramble

Eggs Scrambled with meat and vegetables

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sausage
  • Bell Pepper
  • Onion
  • Bacl pepper and Cummin

Steps

  1. Cook sausage
  2. Add onion and bell pepper to sausage after it has cooked through
  3. Beat eggs and add black pepper and cummin to beaten eggs, then add them to sausage and vegetable mix once onion has yellowed
  4. Continue mixing in frying pan so eggs don't stick or burn
  5. Plate after eggs have reached desired level of fluffiness
