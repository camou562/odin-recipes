Pho

Vietnamese Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice Noodles
  • Beef (or meat of choice)
  • Bouillon
  • Green Onion
  • Various herbs and oils

Steps

  1. Bring bouillon to boil
  2. Slice beef very thin
  3. Add choice herbs and oils to boiling bouillon
  4. Bring separate pot of water to boil
  5. Flash boil rice noodles for roughly 5 to 10 seconds
  6. Add noodles and raw thinly sliced beef to soup bowl
  7. Pour boiling bouillon into soup bowl with rice noodles and raw beef
