Rosemary Steak

Steak cooked in butter with Rosemary and Garlic

Ingredients

  • Steak (NY Strip, Filet Mignon, or Porterhouse)
  • Butter
  • Garlic (sliced)
  • Rosemary
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Add oil to preheated pan, add black pepper to steak
  2. Sear both sides of steak
  3. Add gratuitous amount of butter directly onto pan, not onto steak
  4. Once butter melts, add garlic and rosemary to pan
  5. Use spoon to scoop melted butter onto top of steak
  6. Flip steak and continue with topping with melted butter mixure, adding more butter as needed
  7. Plate steak once desired level of cook is achieved
